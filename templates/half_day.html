Spice Tour

This tour program builds experience and enjoyment of Zanzibar tropical fruits, trees and different spices such as Turmeric, Cardamom, Cinnamon, Black Peppers, Cloves, Nutmegs, and others just to mention are few that are found in Zanzibar Island.

It is important to note that Zanzibar is also very famous as "Spice Island" why because of its historical production of different spices and again its ability to grow variety spice in now days.

Activities

See, taste and smell variety spices found in Zanzibar Island.


Experience the taste of tropical fruits found in Zanzibar


Get spice lunch with unique tropical fruits of Zanzibar Island.


Spice Local market shopping (at the end of tour).



This tour will take almost 2hrs/3hrs with short walking Safari together with our local expert tour guide.

Prison Island Tour

Involve safari to visit best small island of Zanzibar that are found few meters from FORODHANI FISH MARKET at Zanzibar stone town beaches (just off Stone Town).

This Island is also known as Changuu Island by the Local peoples, because to them the word “Changuu” from their local language is common used to mean the popular fishes around the island.

Activities

Experience the story and magic of Prison Island


See Giant Tortoises, who can live up about 200 years


Explore and learn the history of these beautiful creatures


Spending, relaxing and do swimming on the beach of island


Experience good view and take photos of stones towns

The tour will involve sailing around mangroves and snorkeling along Menai bay, which is one of the best coral reefs in Zanzibar.

Activities

Swimming and snorkeling


Visit Kwale Island, natural Lagoon & Sandbank

Fresh fruit tasting like Banana, Watermelon, Pineapple, Mangoes


Sailing with Traditional boat


Witnessing countless colorful fishes and other sea creatures that survive among the coral reefs and underwater plants which together form the barrier reef ecosystem in the bay

Jozani Forest Reserve Tour

This is the biggest forest reserve in Zanzibar Island. Its home of variety amazing species of animals and plants like Red colobus monkeys and mangrove trees

It is important to understand that these monkey species that are found at Jozani Forest Park is unique, only found in Zanzibar Islands you cannot find elsewhere in the world

The approximate number of these monkeys is 6000, with half found in Jozani Forest and another Half can be found in other small forests in Zanzibar Islands. Not only Red Colobus Monkey, there are so many other animals and rare medicinal plants you can find at Jozani Forest.

Activities

Learning about Red colobus monkeys and fascinating Forest of Jozani


Gain insight and knowledge about Swahili tradition medicine plants



This tour will take almost 2hrs/3hrs with short walking Safari together with our local expert tour guide.
