PARLIAMENT TOUR-URT

This tour program is special for client who need to visit any institutions that are found in Tanzania such as universities, colleges, institutes, parliament, ministries, NGOs and religion organizations as well as among others for leisure, business and learning purposes.

Parliament safari

This tour program provide opportunities to the schools, universities, students and even local citizens who are willing to visit the parliament of united republic of Tanzania to learn experience and see how various activities at the parliament being conducted at daily life to fulfil the needs of Tanzania citizens.

It is important to note that, a parliament tour is not only just a parliament safari it is the tour program that also give customer chance to request meeting with their representative of parliaments.

Again, with this tour program a client will be able to enjoy and experience many activities such as learning, charity, debating, and among others in the tour program.
