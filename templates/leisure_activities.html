Study Tour and Excursion

To us, study tour means a trip or tour taken by a group of people to study or investigate on something, such as a culture, sports, nature, wildlife and history.

With Abumariam Safari Island, this tour program aims to build experience, learning and understanding about Tanzania tourism attractions, people and their culture, environment and its resources. The schools, university and even organized groups aiming to undertake study tour will get chance to visit various destinations for study purpose.

Where we are going to do study tour? To us, study tours will be conducted in cultural and natural sites including schools, national parks, museums, paleo-ontological and historical sites, game reserves and controlled areas, and marine parks and reserves. Also, customer can choose study tour sites and inform us to plan or prepare a tour package

It is important to note that study tours are not only for studying purposes, but study tours will include many leisure, recreation, and enjoyment activities such as game viewing, birds watching, cultural tourism, shopping, swimming, arts and crafting, dancing, and among other that will help client to enjoy tour at the maximum.
