Grape farming tour

This tour involve visiting grapes farms that are found in Dodoma. It is important to note that Dodoma region is the second largest producer of wine in sub-Saharan Africa after South Africa. There are three types of grape fruitsgrowing here in Dodoma includes Cabernet Sauvignon, Chenin Blanc and Syrah. Rock arts paintings tour- kondoa irangi:

This tour program involves visiting Kondoa Irangi’s rock art paintings. This is the most magic and wonderful destination, which is a UNESCO World Heritage Site portraying impressive collection of pre-historic rock art. It is a place where one can experience one of the world most ancient examples of human artistic expression showing pre-historic activities such as hunting, and playing music instruments, and interactions of people and animals like elephants, giraffe and antelopes. Most interesting stories of this site is the ability of ancient African societies to artistically display their daily live activities and environment by painting on rocks using locally available materials. The paintings show animals, people, crafts, activities, and instruments, some of which still exist till now

Leisure, recreational and enjoyment activity activities at the tour includes

  • Learning local ways of life

  • Explore rock paintings
  • Village tour
  • Traditional arts and dance
  • Enjoy traditional food
  • Learn about local people and their culture
  • Visiting University of Dodoma
  • See and enjoy Tanzania’s parliament
  • Visiting Nyerere Square Memorial place

Traditional food dishes tour

This tour program involve tour trip to visit, taste, and experience Tanzania traditional food dishes and cuisine. With this tour client, get opportunities to learn Tanzania local foods and drinks through practical knowledge.

Therefore, various Tanzania favorites traditional food dishes such as pilau kuku (to mean seasoned rice with chicken), grilled meat, plantains, beans, flatbread, hot sauce, coconut rice (fragrant rice dish cooked in coconut milk and water and seasoned simply with salt) ugali and among others. These are the some just to mention a few

It is important to note that Tanzanian cuisine is diverse. The types of ingredients used and preparation methods vary greatly from region to region. Spicy foods are common and ingredients like ginger, cinnamon, cloves, and coconut milk are used in many dishes. Why because Tanzanian food dishes most has been significantly influenced by foreign cuisine example Indian cuisine. But this reason not finish the taste of Tanzania traditional foods way prepared is very different from other areas of the world. That is why still new experience and stories to the tourist at all corner of the world.
