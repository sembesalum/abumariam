About Zanzibar

Welcome to Zanzibar

Zanzibar Island also known as Zanzibar archipelago is the part of Tanzania country in East Africa Coast. Zanzibar is a semi-autonomous state within the United Republic of Tanzania, why because Zanzibar united with Tanganyika on 26 April 1964 to form Tanzania.

Historically, Zanzibar is not just a single island, is the combination of two large islands UNGUJA and PEMBA. UNGUJA is the main island in Zanzibar and considered most of the biggest island in Zanzibar archipelago and in addition, it is where the capital city of Zanzibar found. Apart of these two biggest island in Zanzibar, that is UNGUJA and PEMBA but also there are small islands more than 70 found within the Zanzibar archipelago. Many of these small islands not habited by people but mostly used for recreations, enjoyment and other tourism purpose. Example of such small island include

Changuu island (Prison Island)

Mnemba Island

Chumbe Island

Bawe Island

Language

Swahili, is the national language, is a composite of several Bantu dialects and Arabic that originated along the East African coast and on the island of Zanzibar. But most interesting to Zanzibar island is that it is the place that you can find a lot of language spoken by local tour guide expert like English, France, Portuguese, Russian, Italian, Poland, Serbia, Spanish, Germany, Chinese and among other. So saying Zanzibar it is the only destination in world that a visitor can choose guide to his or her own language.
