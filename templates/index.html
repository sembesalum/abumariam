About Us

Abu Mariam Safari Island is a program responsible for creating awareness about Tanzania tourism destinations for both international and domestic tourists.

Important to note that Tanzania endowed with a lot of cultural and natural heritage attractions such as national parks, game reserves, wilderness areas, quality beaches, archaeological and historical sites, museums, monuments, marine reserves, forest reserves, mountains and among others which act as among of the best attractions in the world and their explorations give experiences that tourists may never seen or explored before in their life experience

The Abu Mariam Safari Island, therefore, aims to present new information and updates on everyday unique tourist’s destinations and their diverse tourism attractions such as unique and endemic animal and plant species, best cultural and natural heritage attractions including historical and paleo-ontological sites, wildlife parks and reserves, mountain hiking, coastal and marine sites.

Services
  • Current updates, news, and different articles about tourism attractions and activities in Tanzania.
  • Prepare tour packages for tourists pursuing leisure, businesses or study in Tanzania.
  • Prepare and advise on holiday packages for both domestic and international visitors planning to visit Tanzania tourist destinations.
  • Organize Charity program and safaris for both international and local visitors.
  • Organize rural and urban tour program
  • Organize learning tour packages (visiting institutions (parliament, universities), project sites), etc.
